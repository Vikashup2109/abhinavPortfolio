chapters of my journey

July 2019
After completing my intermediate schooling in a small town, I ventured to Kota, preparing for the JEE exam. Beyond the subjects, Kota taught me the art of problem-solving, sharpening my mind and fueling my love for tackling complex challenges.
october 2020
The pandemic brought the world to a halt, but for me, it was a time of self-discovery. Post my JEE in September, I found myself at a crossroads. The results in November led me to one of the prestigious engineering colleges of India. But my heart yearned for more.
december 2020
I dabbled in Photoshop, creating graphics and double exposure images. My passion grew, leading me to web design. I immersed myself in learning - from YouTube, articles, and a Udemy course on "Master Web Design in Photoshop". This was also when I embarked on my freelancing journey with Designcrowd.
The initial days were tough. After participating in numerous contests without a win, I began to grasp the competitive nature of the platform. But perseverance paid off. After participation in 36 contests I won my first oneü•π. And then I continued finally after 64 contests 4 of which I won, I took rest from crowdsourcing platforms.
July 2021
With newfound confidence and skills, I built my website, showcasing my work. This caught the attention of Growspace's Founder and CEO, leading to my first corporate stint in July. For six months, I learned a lot and got great learning and working experience.
January 2022
January saw me joining Urban Culture as a founding designer. Here, I not only contributed to design but also shaped the company's culture. Here I could merged my love for design with my passion for understanding human behaviour. Regular sessions on creativity, philosophy, and human behaviour became the highlight of my role, where all team members gathered to listen me talk about design and psychology.
2023 & Today
As I continue my role as a founding and senior designer at Urban Culture, this year has been about broadening my impact. I've had the privilege of conducting three workshops, engaging with over 4,500 enthusiastic participants.‚Ä®‚Ä®

Also I've ventured into the world of writing. Sharing insights on human psychology, behavior, and UX design on LinkedIn has become a passion. Each article is a reflection of my journey, my learnings, and my vision for the future of design.
Thanks so much for stopping by!
